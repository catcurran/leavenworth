Visit Leavenworth, WA

Bavarian-themed architecture, stunning mountain scenery, and a wealth of outdoor recreational activities

Top three activities to do at Leavenworth

Adventure

Leavenworth is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts with hiking, backpacking, and snow activities in the mountains and water activities on the Wenatchee River.

Events

Leavenworth hosts numerous festivals and events throughout the year, drawing visitors from near and far.

Food & Shopping

The streets of Leavenworth are lined with boutique shops, offering everything from handcrafted souvenirs to specialty foods.
